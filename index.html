    Our Specialization

    The E-permit compliance manager is a web-based portal created specifically for motor carrier compliance managers, administrators, and safety personnel. Whether you are a for-hire trucking company, private goods hauler or a charter bus operator this web-based tool is for you.


    My E-Permit Book allows you to organize, maintain, renew, share and distribute company, driver and vehicle authorities, licenses, permits, titles, plating credentials and motor carrier specific tax documents and information. Managing your fleets compliance responsibilities has never been easier!

    Create a digital library of company, driver and vehicle compliance documentation and access it from anywhere, anytime.

    Store, archive & maintain documentation to assist with record keeping responsibilities imposed under the International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) and the International Registration Program (IRP).

    Store, Track & Manage Your:

    Corporate Information

    All corporate level information including account numbers and a document listing of business licences and authorities.

    Drivers

    A complete list of your active and non-active driver’s. Includes a document library of driver qualification, license, drug and alcohol, training and other required documents.

    Assets

    Manage and store pertinent information and documents for your fleets active and non-active trucks, tractors, buses, trailers and service vehicles (assets).

    IRP

    This area contains all information and documentation relevant to your company’s enrollment in the International Registration Plan (IRP). Maintain fleets, create weight groups and store your IRP cab cards, fee notices and more.

    IFTA

    This area contains your current and prior years International Fuel Tax Agreement (IFTA) licences together with a decal assignment listing.

    IRS (HVUT)

    This area contains your current and prior year IRS Form 2290 Heavy Vehicle Use Tax Returns and Equipment Schedules. Set an alert to ensure this is renewed on time!
